"UA: First" will no longer show biathlon in Ukraine: who's the new translator

The President of the Biathlon Federation of Ukraine, Vladimir Brynzak, said that negotiations are under way on the broadcasting of the World Cup races in the 2018/2019 season.

The Biathlon World Cup in the season 2018/2019 will be broadcasted by the Inter and NTN channels, according to biathlon.com.ua.

Changed the rules of individual race at the World Cup

The Congress of the International Biathlon Union (IBU) on Saturday amended the rules for the individual race. The organization reduced the fine to 45 seconds for a miss committed under adverse weather conditions.

The penalty minute will be reduced in case of rain, high positive temperature and other factors affecting the quality of the snow cover of the track.

Who will represent Ukraine at the World Cup stage in Pokljuka

Andrei Prokunin, the head coach of the women's national team of Ukraine, told biathlon.com.ua who would participate in the women's national team in a comment.

Regarding the girls who will perform, the coaching staff decided on the names of five athletes. They are Valya and Vita Semerenko, Julia Jima, Elena Pidgrushnaya and Anastasia Merkushina. Another biathlete who will go to Pokljuka will be determined later.
